Overview of Current Projects

Accountable and Fair Computing
Software has become an integral part of our society, impacting many aspects of our daily lives such as health care, criminal justice, education, and social services. Although modern software provides many advantages, there are growing ethical and legal concerns regarding the repercussions of software-based decisions such as legal compliance and fairness.
This project aims to leverage tools and techniques from software engineering, machine learning, and social science to address ethical and legal concerns in cyber systems under uncertainty.
See this presentation, and Parfait-ML and TenForty papers for more information.
